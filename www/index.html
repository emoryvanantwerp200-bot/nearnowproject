Why NearNow instead of 5 separate apps?

One feed instead of constant app switching

  • One live feed for weather, crime, traffic, emergencies, and community updates.
  • Distance-ranked relevance so nearby events stay on top.
  • Real-time local context without tab-hopping.

Built for local relevance

  • Custom categories and quiet hours to reduce noise.
  • Radius controls from half a mile to ten miles.
  • Travel mode behavior by updating to current area.

Key use cases

Daily neighborhood awareness

See what is happening near home and work without checking separate city, weather, and social apps.

Commute and route planning

Spot nearby incidents and weather disruptions that affect drive time and detours.

Family and community safety

Coordinate trusted family circles, share useful reports, and reduce surprise incidents around loved ones.

AI for family circles, not public tracking

NearNow Circle uses AI to turn natural updates into structured, private family statuses. A parent can type "Mom is leaving work and picking up groceries" and NearNow can update the circle card, note, and approximate context for trusted members.

What AI Quick Track does

AI helps organize updates that family members already choose to share. It is designed for convenience, not surveillance.

  • Extracts name, status, note, and location context from a check-in message.
  • Updates an existing family member when the name matches your circle.
  • Labels AI-assisted updates so families know what changed.
  • Keeps circle tracking opt-in and separate from public community reports.

Privacy and trust

How alerts are sourced

NearNow combines public safety data, weather alerts, traffic incidents, breaking news, and verified community reports.

Community reports are reviewed and labeled separately from official alerts.

Your privacy

Your precise location stays on your device unless you explicitly choose to share a report or check in with your private family circle.

  • No always-on public location sharing.
  • No resale of precise location data.
  • Family-circle updates stay separate from public local reports.
  • Delete request support through the contact channel.

Privacy policy summary

What NearNow does not do

  • Does not replace emergency services or 911.
  • Does not treat community reports as official alerts.
  • Does not share family-circle locations publicly.

Frequently asked questions

How is NearNow different from neighborhood apps?

NearNow merges crime, weather, traffic, emergency, news, and community updates into one distance-ranked feed.

Does NearNow track my location?

NearNow uses location on-device for relevance. Precise location stays on-device unless you choose to include it in a report.

Where do alerts come from?

Alerts are sourced from public safety feeds, weather alerts, traffic incidents, breaking news, and verified community reports.

Can users choose which alerts they get?

Yes. Users can set categories, radius, and quiet hours to control what appears and when notifications arrive.

Does NearNow work while traveling?

Yes. NearNow updates based on current area and radius preferences, so it can be used at home or on the road.

Are community reports verified?

Community reports are reviewed and clearly labeled separately from official alerts.

Is NearNow free?

Early beta access is free, and early users receive priority invites.
