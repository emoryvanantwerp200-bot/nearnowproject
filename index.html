Phase 2: Build Trust

Every alert shows why people should believe it.

V

Verified sources

Official agencies, local outlets, schools, public safety feeds, and approved community moderators are labeled clearly.

T

Time stamps

Alerts show when they were posted and when the source last changed, so stale information is easy to spot.

L

Original links

Each summary keeps a path back to source material instead of asking users to trust a black box.

B

Report badges

Community reports move from unreviewed to confirmed only after a trusted source or local moderator verifies them.
